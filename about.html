About me

Front-end developer from Kempton Park, South Africa

Hello there! I'm Melisa Moyo, an enthusiastic front-end developer hailing from South Africa. Armed with a foundation in Business Management and an insatiable curiosity for all things related to Information Technology, I set forth on a thrilling expedition into the realm of programming. The crossing of paths between my business acumen and technological fascination has uniquely shaped my outlook. It equips me not only to craft captivating web experiences but also to comprehend the all-encompassing influence of technology on both businesses and users, painting a holistic picture of its transformative power. My journey began with HTML, a gateway into the realm of crafting digital narratives. As I ventured deeper into the code, my passion grew in parallel with my proficiency. Exploring the intricate dance of HTML tags and elements opened my eyes to the foundational language that underpins the web. This initial spark kindled a desire to bring designs to life, using CSS to weave styles into the fabric of the digital canvas. With each line of code, I uncovered the art of balancing aesthetic appeal and user experience, a journey that continues to evolve with every project I undertake.
